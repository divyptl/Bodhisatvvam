Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Shree Bodhisatvvam ships all physical products across India. We take great care in packaging our handcrafted wellness products to ensure they arrive safely at your doorstep.

1. Shipping Coverage

We currently ship to all serviceable pin codes across India via trusted courier partners. International shipping is not available at this time.

2. Estimated Delivery Timelines

Location Estimated Delivery
Gujarat (within state) 3–5 business days
Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, etc.) 5–7 business days
Other locations across India 7–10 business days
Remote / rural areas 10–14 business days

Note: Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to courier delays, weather, or unforeseen circumstances. We are not responsible for delays caused by the shipping carrier.

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, are calculated at checkout and displayed before payment. We may offer free shipping on orders above a certain value — any such promotions will be clearly mentioned on the website.

4. Order Processing

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days of payment confirmation.
  • Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.
  • Once your order is shipped, you will receive a notification via WhatsApp with tracking information (where available).

5. Order Tracking

You can track your order status through our website using your Order ID. We also send shipping updates via WhatsApp to the number provided during checkout.

6. Delivery of Coaching Sessions

Online coaching sessions (Life Coaching, NLP Training, Mindfulness, etc.) are delivered via video call. No physical shipment is involved. You will receive the video call link on WhatsApp at least 15 minutes before your scheduled session time.

7. Damaged / Missing Products

If your order arrives damaged or if any items are missing, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos and your order ID. We will arrange a replacement or refund as per our Refund & Cancellation Policy.
