Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At Shree Bodhisatvvam, we strive to ensure complete satisfaction with every order and session. This policy outlines our refund and cancellation terms for both physical products and online healing sessions.

1. Refund Policy for Physical Products

Scenario Refund Eligibility Timeline
Damaged/defective product received Full refund or free replacement 5–7 business days after verification
Wrong product delivered Full refund or correct product shipped 5–7 business days after verification
Order not received within estimated time Full refund if not delivered within 15 days 5–7 business days after confirmation
Change of mind (unused, sealed product) Refund minus shipping charges 7–10 business days after product return

To initiate a refund: Contact us via WhatsApp at +91 9737171090 or email shreebodhisatvvam@gmail.com within 7 days of delivery with your order ID and photos/description of the issue.

Non-refundable items: Opened or used products, custom/personalized orders, and products damaged by misuse are not eligible for refunds.

2. Cancellation Policy for Products

  • Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placing them by contacting us on WhatsApp.
  • Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled. You may request a return after delivery (see above).
  • Cancellations are processed as full refunds within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

3. Refund & Cancellation Policy for Coaching Sessions

  • Cancellation 24+ hours before session: Full refund within 5–7 business days.
  • Cancellation less than 24 hours before session: 50% refund or one free reschedule.
  • No-show: No refund will be issued for missed sessions without prior intimation.
  • Free consultation sessions: Can be cancelled or rescheduled any time — no charges apply.
  • No-show: No refund will be issued for missed sessions without prior intimation.
  • Free consultation sessions: Can be cancelled or rescheduled any time — no charges apply.

4. Refund Method

All refunds are processed through Razorpay to the original payment method (UPI, card, net banking, or wallet). Refund processing typically takes 5–7 business days, though your bank may take an additional 2–3 days to reflect the amount.
